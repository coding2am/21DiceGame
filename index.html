Game Explainition

  1. This is a 21 dice throw game. ( It's similar to the 21Card Game )
  2. You need to get a score of 21 by throwing dices
  3. If your score is over the 21 you will be lost and player 2 is an auto winner.
  4. If both of you get the same 21 scores it will be a draw.
